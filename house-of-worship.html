A LED video wall is a large, flat-panel display made up of many small, individual light-emitting diode (LED) modules. These modules are typically mounted in a rectangular array on a metal frame and connected to an electronic controller. The controller allows the operator to control the overall brightness and color of the display.
Houses of worship can incorporate a Led Video Wall into multiple facets of their religious practices, like the presentation of song lyrics and sermon highlights, streaming live videos, and host virtual guest appearances.
